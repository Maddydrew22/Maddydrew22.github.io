SHARE Day Camp 2020

For more than 30 years, The Salvation Army’s summer SHARE program has provided a safe, educational, and God-centered experience for the children of Midland. SHARE is a drop in summer day camp for kids entering Kindergarten through the 5th grade. This exciting program is open to all families, regardless of socioeconomic status.  

SHARE Dates:     Session I: June 15th- July 10th          Session II: July 20th – August 13th

***Dates subject to change pending the Midland Public School Calendar***

SHARE Program: Monday – Friday 9:30am-3:30pm     Last Day of Session II: THURSDAY, August 13th

SHARE Before/After Care: Monday – Friday 7:30am-9:30am; 3:30pm-5:30pm

Registration fee: $10 per session and a $5.00 camp shirt fee due at registration – Camp Fee: $40 per week; Before/After Care: $25 per week

Fees are due the Friday before services are rendered.

Eligibility Guidelines

  • Applications can be returned to 330 Waldo Ave. If mailing, please include registration fee with the application.

The Salvation Army Mission Statement

The Salvation Army, an international movement, is an evangelical part of the universal Christian Church. Its message is based on the Bible. Its ministry is motivated by the love of God. Its mission is to preach the gospel of Jesus Christ and to meet human needs in His name without discrimination.
